The Through Line

UX strategy is synthesis — bringing research, design, content, and product thinking into a coherent point of view a client can act on. My job is to build the team and conditions that make that synthesis possible.

But you can't find the through lines if people aren't telling you what's actually happening. The synthesis only works if the information is honest.

Success Criteria
Design reviews completed and shared with PM before sprint handoff — no engineering blockers
All requests acknowledged within one business day, even if full response requires more time

Scenario 3 — AI Integration

Anchor on concepts, not products.

Common Mistake

Chase tools. Rebuild assumptions every cycle. Make infrastructure decisions based on the latest release.

The Approach

Anchor on concepts. What is an LLM good at? Where does it fail? Build the foundation that lets your team evaluate any tool — not just today's.

Scenario 3 — AI Integration

We can prototype fast. Then we get stuck.

Where We Gained

High-fidelity in hours, not days. Stakeholders react to something real. Alignment happens faster.

Where We Got Stuck

Translating the prototype into our actual design system. Across multiple products, multiple teams, multiple pattern libraries. Still too slow.
